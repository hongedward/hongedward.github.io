WELCOME
Lamppost is a community for anime fans who want to share recommendations with one another, in the form of rankings lists we call "Posts". Named after the ubiquitous structures that people post information on, Lamppost is designed to shine a light on users' opinions and introduce visitors to their next favorite show. If you're new here, read on! If you have an account, Log In!

Explore.

Scroll down to find our Featured Posts. Use the search bar at the top of the page to find a certain series or user you'd like to know more about. An Advanced Search can be found if you hover over the "Lamppost" in the topbar. Advanced options allow to you limit search results by genre, series length, staff member, and more.

Create.

Love the Posts you see? Sign Up and make your own! Lamppost has premade templates to help you create a gorgeous Post. All you have to do is select what type of information you'd like to include for each series on your list, upload pictures, then write away! You can always edit your Posts later should you want to add/delete information or change the layout of the list.

Interact.

Leave a Comment or Rate a Post to let users know how you feel about their list, or use the social media buttons at the end of each Post to quickly share a link through Facebook or Twitter! Follow the users whose tastes you really like, Message them directly - just avoid breaking any of our Rules of Engagement.
